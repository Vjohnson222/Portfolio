ABOUT


I am a talented web designer and developer with a passion for creating visually appealing and functional websites.

As a mother of two amazing young men, I understand the importance of balancing work and personal life. In my free time, I enjoy staying active and exploring my creative side through professional concert dance, building functional furniture, and hiking.

My love for dance has allowed me to hone my sense of rhythm and movement, while my furniture building skills have taught me the value of precision and attention to detail. These diverse interests have all helped shape me into the well-rounded web developer I am today.


I am dedicated to creating websites that are both aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly. I am super excited to continue growing my skills and working on more projects in the field.
